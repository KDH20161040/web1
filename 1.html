HTML

HyperText MarkupLanguage (HyperText Markup Language, HTML, Cultural Language: Super text marker language, hyper-textmarker language) is the dominant mark-up languagefor web pages.

HTML provides a way to create structured documentswith links, quotations, and other items, as well as to represent the structural meaning semantics for the body, such as titles, paragraphs, lists, and other items.
